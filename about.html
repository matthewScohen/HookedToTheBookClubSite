What do we do?

We read books! For fun! And less stress!

  • General Format
    • 4 books a month (You are not required to read any! You can still come to meetings if you haven’t read a word!)
    • We will pick one of the four books each month to be the “spoiler book”
    • First 30 minutes: Announcements/introductions/general conversations
    • Second 30 minutes: Discussion of the spoiler book. There WILL BE SPOILERS. This is why it is called the SPOILER BOOK!
